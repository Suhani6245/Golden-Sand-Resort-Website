Welcome to Golden Sand Resort - Your Dream Destination Awaits!

Welcome to Golden Sand Resort, a perfect blend of luxury and natural beauty. Located on a stunning beachfront, our resort offers breathtaking ocean views and direct access to golden sands. Enjoy a wide range of accommodations, from stylish rooms to private villas, all designed for ultimate comfort. Golden Sand Resort Policy

  1. Check-In/Check-Out
    • Check-in: 3:00 PM
    • Check-out: 11:00 AM
    • Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request and subject to availability.
  2. Reservation & Payment
    • A valid credit card is required for booking and will be charged at the time of reservation.
    • Full payment is required upon check-in for all reservations.
  3. Cancellation Policy
    • Cancellations made within 48 hours of arrival will incur a 1-night charge.
    • No-show or early departure may result in full charge for the reserved stay.
  4. Smoking
    • Smoking is prohibited in all indoor areas. Designated smoking areas are available.
  5. Pets
    • Pets are allowed.
  6. Quiet Hours
    • Quite hours are from 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM to ensure a peacefu; environment for all guests.
  7. Pool & Spa Use
    • Pool and spa are for registered guests only.
    • Proper swim attire required. Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult.
  8. Damage or Loss
    • Guests are responsible for any damage or loss to resort property during their stay.
  9. Safety & Security
    • The resort is not responsible for lost or stolen personal belongings.
    • Alcohol consumption is restricted to designated areas.
  10. Behavior & Conduct
    • Disorderly conduct, disruptive behavior, or violation of resort policies may result in eviction without refund.

Golden Sand Resort Cancellation & Refund Policy

Cancellation Notice
Cancellations must be made at least 48 hours prior to check-in to avoid a fee.
Late Cancellation Fees
Cancellations within 48 hours of arrival will incur a 1-night charge.
No-Show Policy
A no-show will result in a full charge for the reserved stay.
Refund Process
Refunds will be issued within 7-10 business days to the original payment method.
Non-Refundable Bookings
Bookings marked as non-refundable are not eligible for any refunds or charges.

For more details or assistance, please contact the Golden Sand Resort front desk.
